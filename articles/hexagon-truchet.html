Simple tile

We start by coming up with code to tile the plane with regular hexagons. I will take inspiration from Triangular Grid Math, but we don't have to worry about upwards- and downwards-directions. No rotations are needed.

With three pairs of curves per tile, we have 5 possible Catalan tiles.

No LLMs were consulted in the making of this site.
